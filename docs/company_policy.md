# Company Policies — Avivo Inc.

## Return Policy
Customers may return any product within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. To initiate a return, contact support@avivo.com with your order number.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. All remote employees must be available on Slack between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM in their local timezone. VPN usage is mandatory when accessing internal systems.

## Code of Conduct
Avivo employees are expected to treat all colleagues, customers, and partners with respect. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated and may result in immediate termination.

## Data Privacy
All customer data is stored securely and encrypted at rest (AES-256). Avivo does not sell or share personal data with third parties. Data retention period is 7 years for financial records and 2 years for usage logs.

## Expense Reimbursement
Employees can submit expense claims up to $500 per month without manager pre-approval. Claims above this limit require written approval from a department head before expenditure.
