# Avivo Product Guide

## Avivo Core (Free Tier)
The Core plan is completely free and includes access to up to 3 projects, 500 MB of storage, basic analytics dashboard, and community support. Ideal for individual developers and small experiments.

## Avivo Pro ($49/month)
Pro includes unlimited projects, 50 GB of storage, advanced analytics with custom dashboards, webhook integrations, priority email support with 24-hour response time, and a 99.9% uptime SLA.

## Avivo Enterprise (Custom Pricing)
Enterprise customers receive dedicated infrastructure, SSO/SAML 2.0 integration, custom rate limits, a dedicated account manager, SLA with credits, on-premise deployment options, and compliance reports (SOC2, ISO 27001).

## Avivo SDK
The Avivo SDK is available for Python, Node.js, and Go. Install via:
- Python: `pip install avivo-sdk`
- Node.js: `npm install @avivo/sdk`
- Go: `go get github.com/avivo/sdk-go`

The SDK auto-handles authentication, retries with exponential backoff, and response pagination.

## Billing & Payments
Billing cycles are monthly. Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and bank transfers (ACH/SEPA) are accepted. Invoices are emailed automatically. To upgrade, downgrade, or cancel, go to Settings → Billing.

## Getting Support
- Free users: community forum at community.avivo.com
- Pro users: email support@avivo.com (24h response)
- Enterprise: dedicated Slack channel + phone support
