# Quick Recipes — Avivo Kitchen Guide

## Classic Tomato Pasta
**Serves:** 2 | **Time:** 20 minutes

Ingredients: 200g spaghetti, 400g canned crushed tomatoes, 2 cloves garlic, 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, fresh basil.

Instructions: Boil salted water and cook pasta until al dente. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat, add minced garlic and sauté 1 minute. Add crushed tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, simmer 10 minutes. Toss pasta in sauce. Top with fresh basil leaves.

## Avocado Toast with Egg
**Serves:** 1 | **Time:** 10 minutes

Ingredients: 1 slice sourdough bread, half an avocado, 1 egg, lemon juice, chili flakes, salt.

Instructions: Toast the bread. Mash avocado with lemon juice and salt. Fry or poach the egg. Spread avocado on toast, top with egg, sprinkle chili flakes.

## Overnight Oats
**Serves:** 1 | **Time:** 5 minutes prep + overnight

Ingredients: 80g rolled oats, 200ml milk (or oat milk), 1 tbsp chia seeds, 1 tsp honey, fresh berries.

Instructions: Mix oats, milk, chia seeds, and honey in a jar. Refrigerate overnight. In the morning, top with fresh berries and serve cold.

## Lemon Ginger Tea
**Serves:** 1 | **Time:** 5 minutes

Ingredients: 250ml boiling water, 1cm fresh ginger (sliced), half a lemon (juiced), 1 tsp honey.

Instructions: Add ginger slices to boiling water. Steep 3–5 minutes. Remove ginger. Add lemon juice and honey. Stir and serve.
